Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

RootCall (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our AI call screening and protection services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as your name, phone number, and email.
  • Call metadata (time, caller ID, duration) — never your private conversations.
  • Billing data processed securely through our payment partner (Stripe).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To operate and improve our AI call protection features.
  • To detect and block scam or fraudulent activity.
  • To provide customer support and account management.

3. Data Protection

Your data is stored securely using encryption and is never sold or shared with third parties except when required by law or for essential service operations.

4. Payments

We do not store payment information. All transactions are processed through Stripe with PCI compliance.

5. Contact

For questions, email us at support@getrootcall.com.
